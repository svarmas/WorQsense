How we are different

The standard the work demands.

AWI Certificate Holder (AWI-CH)

Every investigation led by Sreevidya Varma, an Association of Workplace Investigators Certificate Holder, the internationally recognised benchmark for workplace investigation competence. awi.org ↗

Defensible findings

Not a report. A record: evidence mapped, inference documented, credibility assessed. Built to survive tribunal scrutiny, appellate challenge, and board review.

True independence

No local referral relationships, no proximity pressure, no entanglements. The independence is structural, not aspirational, particularly relevant for India engagements.

Psychosocial safety

Trauma-aware interviewing across every engagement. The investigation process itself must not cause harm. Applied as non-negotiable methodology throughout.

Two lenses, one practice

Investigations answer what happened. Systems intelligence answers what produced it. Both lenses, in every engagement, so findings are defensible and conditions are visible.

IMS methodology

PEACE model, Mendez Principles, TILES System® via IMS, Australia. Evidence-based, contextualised for workplace settings, not adapted from law enforcement.
