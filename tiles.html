TILES System® · IMS Australia

The interview is where investigations succeed or fail.

TILES System® is the world's first investigative interview management software. It replaces paper plans and Word documents with a structured, court-admissible digital record — from the first interview to the final report. Available for Indian organisations through WORQsense.

Get access Why it matters
The problem with paper

Most IC and GRC interviews are planned in Word and conducted with a notebook. That record will not survive scrutiny.

When a finding is challenged in court, appellate review, or before the board, the interview record is examined first. Gaps, inconsistencies, and the absence of a structured plan are the first things an opposing counsel looks for.

TILES places a digital footprint across the entire investigative interviewing workflow: pre-interview planning, in-interview execution, post-interview evaluation. Every action within the system is recorded in a digitally signed, read-only Forensic Log — making the record of the interview itself court-admissible and forensically robust.

For Indian organisations operating under the 90-day PoSH timeline, or the GRC inquiry requirements under the Industrial Relations Code 2020, this is not optional rigour. It is what the process demands.

What TILES does

End-to-end management of your investigative interviewing workflow.

From interview planning to post-interview evaluation, TILES replaces fragmented, paper-based processes with a single, secure, structured digital workflow.

01
Strategic interview planning

Plan each interview using structured topic management — Occurrence, Person, Place, Evidential and more. Map objectives to each phase before entering the room. Add tactical notes and link documentary evidence directly to interview objectives.

02
Integrated mapping & timeline

Build evidence maps and event timelines as interviews progress. Swipe to Timeline™ lets investigators tag statements directly to the chronology in real time — creating an instantly searchable briefing record.

03
Collaboration & supervisory workflow

Multiple investigators, senior investigators, and supervisors can work together across the interviewing workflow, remotely and securely. Role-based access ensures each user sees only what they need to.

04
Forensic interview log

Every action taken within TILES is recorded in a digitally signed, read-only Forensic Log Table. The exported Interview Report and Forensic Log are admissible in court and regulatory proceedings.

05
Enterprise management dashboard

Track interview plans, evaluate investigative outcomes, and measure efficiency across your organisation's investigative interviewing workflow from a single dashboard.

06
Multi-platform & API integration

Browser-based: web, tablet, and mobile (iOS and Android). Integrates with existing HRMS and case management systems via API — no duplicate data entry, no siloed records.

Security & data

Built for high-stakes investigations. Every design decision reflects that.

Records produced by TILES meet the standard required for court and regulatory proceedings — because the system builds rigour in by design, not by effort.

256-bit SSL encryption

Bank-grade SSL data encryption on all transmissions. Hosted on Microsoft Azure cloud infrastructure with 99.99% uptime — servers located in the Netherlands and Australia with automatic routing to the nearest server.

Cyber Essentials Plus certified

Independently verified certification that cybersecurity measures meet rigorous industry standards. Enterprise clients can also deploy TILES on their own servers to comply with in-house IT security governance requirements.

Forensically robust records

Every system action is recorded in a digitally signed, read-only Forensic Log Table. The Interview Report and Forensic Log can be exported and retained for court scrutiny — admissible in proceedings wherever the record is examined.
