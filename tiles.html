Interview management software

From the first conversation to the final report — every interview, managed.

TILES System® is developed by IMS Australia — specialists in investigative interviewing methodology used by law enforcement, regulators, and professional investigators across multiple countries.

For Indian organisations navigating PoSH, GRC, and misconduct investigations, TILES provides what the regulatory environment increasingly demands: rigour that withstands scrutiny.

The methodology behind the software

TILES is built on PEACE and the Mendez Principles. Not just a tool — a practice standard.

TILES embeds investigative interview methodology into the software itself. The PEACE model (Preparation & Planning, Engage & Explain, Account, Closure, Evaluation) and the Mendez Principles for ethical, non-coercive interviewing are not optional add-ons — they structure how interviews are planned, conducted, and recorded.

This matters because methodology reliability is what makes investigation findings defensible. When findings are challenged — in court, in regulatory proceedings, in board review — it is the methodology, not the conclusion, that is examined first.

PEACE model

  • P
    Preparation & PlanningDefine objectives, review evidence, plan the interview structure before entering the room.
  • E
    Engage & ExplainBuild rapport and set the tone. Explain the purpose, the process, and the person's rights.
  • A
    AccountElicit the free-form account. Open questions, active listening, no premature challenge.
  • C
    ClosureClose the interview with care. Confirm understanding, allow final statements, set expectations.
  • E
    EvaluationReview and assess. What did the account reveal? What needs follow-up?
