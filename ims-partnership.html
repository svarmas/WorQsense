The training partner

Why the interview is where investigations succeed or fail.

The interview is where evidence is established or lost. An IC member who doesn't know how to structure a complainant interview cannot produce a defensible record, regardless of how strong the underlying complaint is.

Most IC and HR training covers what the PoSH Act requires: timelines, constitution, documentation. Almost none covers how to actually conduct the inquiry: what to say, how to structure questions, how to handle disclosure, how to build an evidence record from an interview.

Legally compliant but procedurally deficient is still a liability. The procedural deficiency almost always starts in the interview room.

The methodology

Three pillars. One framework.

PEACE Model

The internationally recognised structure for ethical, evidence-based investigative interviews. Preparation and Planning. Engage and Explain. Account. Closure. Evaluate. Provides a structured framework that reduces bias, builds rapport, and produces comprehensive, reliable accounts.

Mendez Principles

Principles on effective interviewing for investigations and information gathering, based on the UN Special Rapporteur's work on non-coercive, rights-respecting interview practice. Applied in WORQsense training to reflect both ethical and evidential standards.

TILES System®

The IMS platform for training delivery, skills assessment, and post-training reinforcement. Participants practice interview technique, receive structured feedback, and are assessed against the methodology. Learning is observable and measurable.

Delivery formats

Three ways to train. One standard.

Online

Virtual delivery

Full programme delivered via secure video: structured modules, live practice interviews, and TILES System® assessment. Accessible from anywhere in India or New Zealand.

Best for: distributed teams, organisations across multiple locations, and where travel is not practical.

Hybrid

Mixed delivery

Foundational modules delivered online; practical interview sessions conducted in-person with direct coaching and feedback. Combines accessibility with hands-on practice.

Best for: teams that benefit from in-person practice while keeping the programme accessible across locations.

Face to Face

In-person delivery

Full programme conducted in-person with WORQsense and IMS facilitators. Intensive practical focus with direct coaching, live interview practice, and group debrief.

Best for: cohorts at a single site, leadership development focus, and where maximum practice time is the priority.
