The productivity cost

Unresolved conduct issues don't stay contained. They spread, through the team, into management bandwidth, and eventually into productivity, retention, and reputation. The system is always more expensive than the symptom.

When to engage

Following an investigation to implement recommendations, or independently, where risk signals have been identified before a formal complaint is raised. Earlier is always less expensive.

The connection to investigations

Every WORQsense investigation includes a systems signals section, what the incident reveals about structural, cultural, and leadership conditions. ReWireWorks takes that signal and works with it.

Diagnostics

See clearly before the incident does it for you.

Structured diagnostic work, grounded in what the investigation found, or what the risk signals are revealing. Available in India and New Zealand.

Culture diagnostics

Structured assessment of organisational culture, risk patterns, and conduct conditions. Seeing clearly before the incident, not after.

Psychosocial safety assessment

Psychosocial safety by design, not as a policy checkbox, but as a structural and leadership condition. In New Zealand, a live obligation under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 and the WorkSafe psychosocial framework.

Leadership alignment diagnostics

Understanding the conditions leadership sets and the signals it sends. Leadership is often the proximate cause of conduct risk; it is always part of the solution.

Prevention & Intervention

Address the conditions. Not just the complaint.

Work grounded in what the diagnostic or investigation revealed, translating findings into structural and cultural change.

Post-investigation structural redesign

Implementing the recommendations that follow an inquiry. Translating findings about conditions into structural and process changes that reduce recurrence.

Team resets

Structured facilitation for teams following an investigation, significant incident, or period of conflict. Not a team-building exercise, a reset grounded in what happened and what needs to change.

Mediation

Where the situation calls for a structured conciliation process rather than, or alongside, a formal inquiry. Led by Sreevidya Varma, AMINZ-credentialled mediator.
