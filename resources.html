Regulatory Guidance

Compliance you can act on

The legal and regulatory framework for workplace investigations in India and New Zealand. What the law actually requires, not what people assume it does.

India · Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act 2013 (PoSH)

The 90-day inquiry requirement

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 requires inquiries to be completed within 90 days. Most Internal Committee members have never conducted an inquiry before.

Investigation support
India · Industrial Relations (IR) Code, 2020

Grievance Redressal Committee obligations

The Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC) under the Industrial Relations Code 2020 operates independently of the Internal Committee. Separate obligations, separate processes. Both require defensible inquiry procedures.

India practice
New Zealand · ERA 2000

Employment Relations Act and good faith

The Employment Relations Act 2000 and the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 set clear expectations for fair process, good faith obligations, and psychosocial risk management. Non-compliance carries direct legal exposure.

New Zealand practice
Investigation Standards

What defensible practice looks like

Not all investigations are created equal. The standard matters, because the findings have legal, regulatory, and human consequences.

AWI · Association of Workplace Investigators

The AWI investigation standard

The AWI Certificate Holder (AWI-CH) credential is the ANSI-accredited benchmark for workplace investigation practice. It defines the standard of care that distinguishes a defensible finding from an exposure. awi.org ↗

IMS · Interview Management Solutions

Evidence-based interviewing methodology

The PEACE model, Méndez Principles, and TILES System® are the global standard for evidence-based, trauma-aware investigative interviews. Developed by Interview Management Solutions (IMS, Australia). interviewmanagementsolutions.com ↗

The IMS partnership
AMINZ · Arbitrators and Mediators Institute of NZ

Section 10 Conciliation

The PoSH Act permits conciliation at the complainant's request. Sreevidya Varma is an AMINZ-credentialed mediator. This is a distinct process, available only in specific circumstances, and conducted to professional mediation standards.
