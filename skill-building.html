IMS partnership

Interview Management Solutions, Australia.

WORQsense delivers investigative interviewing training in partnership with IMS, Australia, built on three foundational frameworks, contextualised for workplace settings.

PEACE model

Structured and ethical

Preparation and planning, Engage and explain, Account, Closure, Evaluation. A structured approach that produces reliable evidence and protects procedural integrity.

Mendez Principles

Trauma-aware

UN principles for effective, humane interview practice. Non-coercive, trauma-informed, designed to elicit accurate accounts without causing harm.

TILES System®

Interview management

A structured system for managing investigative interviews. Developed by IMS, Australia, contextualised for PoSH, GRC, and employment investigations.

In partnership with Interview Management Solutions (IMS, Australia). Evidence-based, not adapted from law enforcement. Designed for workplace and employment relations settings. About the IMS partnership ↗
