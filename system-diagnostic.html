System Diagnostic

Seeing clearly before the next incident, not after. Identifying where the organisation is exposed: in process, policy, culture, and capability, before risk becomes reality.

01 Disputes & Conduct · 02 Capability Boost · 04 System Intervention
Diagnose

Is your culture shaping how work gets done, or explaining why it doesn't?

System diagnostics that identify where the organisation is exposed: gaps in process, policy, culture, and capability that create risk before an incident occurs. The diagnostic draws on investigation findings and ReWireWorks expertise to identify both procedural exposures and the conditions producing them.

Most leaders sense something is wrong before they can name it. The System Diagnostic gives them the language and the map, so they can see clearly and shift deliberately.

Culture diagnostics: what your climate data, surveys, and attrition patterns are actually saying
Policy gap analysis: where your Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act 2013 (PoSH), Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC), and conduct frameworks are exposed
Risk mapping: identifying process, capability, and structural conditions that create incident risk
Team effectiveness assessment: performance, trust, and psychosocial safety at team level
Psychosocial hazard assessment: what conditions are creating harm or eroding safety
Clear diagnostic report: conditions, risk areas, and signals, with practical recommendations

Who this is for

  • Organisations with recurring incidents who want to understand the underlying conditions
  • Leaders who sense something is wrong but cannot see it clearly
  • HR teams preparing for structural or cultural change
  • Boards and leadership teams needing an independent picture of organisational health and risk
  • Organisations subject to GRC requirements under Industrial Relations (IR) Code, 2020 wanting to understand their readiness
