Our investigation standard

Most investigations get filed. Some get challenged. The ones that fail were never built to survive.

The PoSH Act requires inquiries to be completed within 90 days. Most IC members have never conducted an inquiry before.

An investigation that cannot demonstrate procedural integrity: structured interviews, evidence-mapped findings, credibility assessed without bias, is vulnerable the moment it is challenged. At tribunal, at appellate review, or in front of the board.

WORQsense produces a record, not just a report. Evidence mapped to findings. Inferences documented. Credibility assessed. The process is defensible because the methodology is built to that standard from scope-setting.

The inquiry must stand up, at tribunal, in front of the board, and to the people it affects.

Annual IC report obligation

The PoSH Act requires every IC to submit an annual report to the employer and district officer. WORQsense can prepare or review your annual IC report to ensure it is accurate, compliant, and defensible.

Sexual harassment complaints

Investigations under the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. Scoped, conducted, and reported within the 90-day requirement. IC-supported or independently led.

Conciliation

Before an inquiry begins, the complainant may request conciliation. Available only at the complainant's request. Monetary settlement cannot be imposed. Sreevidya Varma is an AMINZ-credentialed mediator.

PEACE model

Preparation and Planning, Engage and Explain, Account, Closure, Evaluate. A structured framework that produces reliable evidence and protects procedural integrity from first contact to final report.

Mendez Principles

UN-grounded principles for non-coercive, trauma-aware interview practice. Applied across every interview in every engagement: complainant, respondent, and witness.

TILES System®

IMS's structured interview management framework. Applied in investigations and taught in WORQsense capability programmes so IC members and HR teams can conduct interviews to the same standard.

When to engage

The situations that call for WORQsense.

Active PoSH complaint (India)

A complaint has been received under the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. The 90-day deadline is running.

GRC matter under IR Code 2020

A Grievance Redressal Committee matter requires investigation. The GRC process is separate from PoSH and requires independent investigative capability.

Misconduct or conduct dispute

A complaint involves bullying, harassment, misconduct, or a conduct dispute where internal investigation is not appropriate due to proximity, seniority, or conflict of interest.

ERA exposure (New Zealand)

A personal grievance, dismissal, or disciplinary matter in New Zealand requires a defensible process under the Employment Relations Act, 2000.

Internal capacity is compromised

The internal team knows the parties. Or the parties know each other. Or leadership is implicated. Independence requires going outside.

A previous investigation is being challenged

A prior investigation is subject to tribunal challenge, appellate review, or board questioning. You need an independent assessment of process and findings.

Annual IC report is due

The annual IC report is a statutory obligation. WORQsense can prepare or review the annual IC report to ensure it is compliant, accurate, and defensible.

Complainant has requested conciliation

Under Section 10 of the PoSH Act, a complainant may request conciliation before an inquiry begins. This requires a credentialed process. Sreevidya Varma is an AMINZ-credentialed mediator.
