Upskill

Teaching the how. Not just the what.

Internal Committee (IC) members, HR teams, and legal committees trained in structured investigative methodology: interview technique, evidence standards, defensible process, and psychosocial safety. Not compliance awareness, but the actual skills required to run an inquiry that stands up.

What an IC member says in the first ten minutes of a complainant interview determines whether the inquiry stands up. Most are never taught what to say. WORQsense and IMS teach it, using evidence-based methods contextualised for Indian workplace and PoSH settings.

Internal Committee (IC) member training: PoSH Act 2013 investigative methodology, interview technique, and evidence standards
Investigative interviewing: PEACE model and Mendez Principles via the TILES System® platform, in partnership with IMS, Australia
HR investigator upskilling: structured process, scope control, and documentation standards
Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC) readiness training aligned to IR Code 2020 requirements
Psychosocial safety in investigations: trauma-aware interviewing and retaliation risk management
Post-training assessment and skills reinforcement via the TILES System® platform
Managers and people leaders: early signal recognition and escalation process
